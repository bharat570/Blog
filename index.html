AI can be Integrated into Robots, machines etc. and making them do things do things that Humans cannot do easily and faster. Understanding Terms that comes under AI:
  1. Machine Learning
    • Machine Learning lets computers learn from data without being programmed step-by-step. Instead of strict instructions, it improves itself by finding patterns in data.
    • A spam filter, for example, learns to detect unwanted emails over time. The more examples it sees, the better it becomes at predictions.
    • Think of it as teaching a machine through examples rather than fixed rules.
  2. Deep Learning
    • Deep Learning is a type of Machine Learning that uses neural networks with many layers. Each layer learns more complex patterns from the data.
    • It powers tech like image recognition, voice assistants, and self-driving cars. It needs large amounts of data and high computing power.
    • You can think of it as teaching machines to “see” and “hear” like humans.
  3. Generative AI
    • Generative AI creates new content — text, images, music, or videos. Instead of only analyzing data, it generates something entirely new.
    • Examples include ChatGPT and Gemini, which create text or art from prompts. It’s widely used in design, marketing, and entertainment.
    • Generative AI doesn’t just “think” — it “creates.”
  4. Data Science
    • Data Science involves collecting, cleaning, and analyzing large amounts of data. It blends statistics, coding, and subject knowledge to make sense of information.
    • Companies use it to improve products, understand customers, and predict trends. It ensures AI systems have quality data to learn from.
    • Without Data Science, AI would have no meaningful knowledge base.
